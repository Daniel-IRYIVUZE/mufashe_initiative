Tips for Returning to School

  1. Research schools with flexible schedules or distance learning options
  2. Inquire about on-campus childcare services
  3. Connect with other student mothers for support
  4. Explore scholarship opportunities specifically for young parents
  5. Don't hesitate to ask for help from teachers and counselors
