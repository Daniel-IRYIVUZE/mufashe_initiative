Why We Exist

Every year, thousands of young women in Rwanda face early pregnancy with fear and uncertainty. Many feel isolated, judged, and without hope for their future.

The Mufashe Initiative was founded in 2018 to address this critical need. Our mission is to provide comprehensive mentorship, mental health support, and educational resources to help young mothers build brighter futures for themselves and their children.
